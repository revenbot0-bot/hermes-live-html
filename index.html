Plain-English conclusion

Most likely: a tense, temporary deal or rolling ceasefire that reduces some shipping pressure but leaves the big disputes unresolved.

Biggest danger: a misread missile/drone incident, a shipping attack, or fighting in Lebanon pulling the wider region back into escalation.

Best-case path: confirmed ceasefire extension, more vessels through Hormuz, phased sanctions relief, and nuclear talks that park the hardest issues instead of letting them explode.
